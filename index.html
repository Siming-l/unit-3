Socioeconomic Development Across the 16 German States

This interactive visualization explores regional socioeconomic development across the 16 states of Germany. The choropleth map and coordinated bar chart support comparison of population, density, HDI, GDP per capita, and unemployment patterns across states.

Data sources include Destatis, Eurostat, Global Data Lab, Bundesagentur für Arbeit, and Eurostat GISCO. This compiled dataset combines the most recent available state-level indicators across multiple years.
